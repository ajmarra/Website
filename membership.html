ACM Membership Guidlines

Requirements to be an active member of the ACM

Payment of dues ($15/semester, venmo or check)
Attend 5 weekly events or have an excused absence for accepted reasons (work, class, etc.)
Membership of one committee and active contribution to the committee

Benefits of Membership in ACM

Weekly general meetings with pizzas (open to all)
Weekly interview prep. (open to all)
Purchasing club shirts (member only)
Inclusion on the published roster of ACM Members (member only)
Attendance to the annual faculty lunch (active member only)
Induction in the ACM (active member only)
Covered fees for the ICPC (antive member only)
Consideration for a yearly ACM scholarship (tbd*, non-leader active member only)
Option to run for executive board positions (active member only)

Please contact exec@wm.acm.org or our secretary to express your interest of joining and we will contact you.


Membership in the national ACM is also encouraged. Student memberships are only $19 (this goes up considerably once you graduate) and provide a wide range of benefits - find more information here. All leaders are required to maintain membership in the national chapter.


*We do not provide refunds of donations.

This information is kept confidential and is not shared.
